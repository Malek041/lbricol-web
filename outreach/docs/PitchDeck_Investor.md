### Standardizing Trust in Morocco's $3B Home Services Market

---

## 🚩 The Problem
### The Informal Economy Gaps

1.  **Fragmentation**: No central hub for vetted, reliable services.
2.  **Haggling Culture**: Inconsistent pricing creates friction and distrust.
3.  **Low Reliability**: High "no-show" rates and zero accountability.
4.  **Operational Chaos**: Gig workers lack tools to manage their income/work.

---

## ⚡ The Solution
### Morocco's First Standardized Marketplace

-   **Instant Access**: Live availability across 80+ service categories.
-   **Transparancy**: Algorithm-driven pricing based on hours, units, or fixes.
-   **Structured Trust**: KYC-vetted professionals (Bricolers) with live ratings.
-   **Payment Efficiency**: Seamless order management and commission handling.

## 🛠️ The Product
### Built for Scale and Simplicity

- **Client App**: Search, book, track, and pay in < 2 minutes.
- **Provider Dashboard**: Lead management, earnings tracking, and professional identity.
- **Service Catalog**: 80+ specialized sub-services (Plumbing to Babysitting).
- **Public Bidding Engine**: Real-time matching for high-complexity logistics/deliveries.

---

## 🚀 Execution & Traction
### Mature Product. Real Presence.

- **Infrastructure**: Full-stack Next.js/Firebase platform live at `lbricol.com`.
- **Coverage**: Operations mapped across **7 major Moroccan cities** (incl. Casablanca, Marrakech, Rabat).
- **Multilingual**: Native Arabic (RTL) support for full population inclusivity.
- **High Repeatability**: Designed for recurring household needs (Cleaning, Maintenance, Errands).

---

## 💰 Business Model
### Healthy Margins. Massive Volume.

-   **Service Fee**: 15% commission on every order.
-   **Unit Economics**: High LTV (Life-Time Value) through recurring maintenance.
-   **Scalability**: Low-overhead digital infrastructure; no inventory.
-   **Expansion Play**: Potential for Fintech (Wallet) and Enterprise integrations.
