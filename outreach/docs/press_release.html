🌍 Morocco's First On-Demand Home Services Marketplace

Digitizing Trust: Lbricol.com Launches Nationwide Home Services Platform

Empowering local artisans and professionals while standardizing quality and safety for millions of households.

CASABLANCA, MOROCCO — Lbricol, a Moroccan-built technology startup, has launched a mobile-first marketplace that connects households with trusted, local service professionals across Morocco. The platform — available today at lbricol.com — covers an extensive range of services including home repairs, cleaning, plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, gardening, babysitting, cooking, and private drivers.

The Marketplace Philosophy

🛠️ Professional Pride

Empowering local talent ("Bricolers") with a digital storefront. We provide the tools for artisans to build a reputation, manage their schedule, and scale their income.

🏠 Household Safety

A rigorous vetting process and a transparent community rating system. Clients can book with confidence, backed by clear pricing and historical performance data.

💡 Modern Efficiency

Moving beyond informal WhatsApp groups and word-of-mouth. Lbricol offers real-time matching, instant messaging, and structured job distribution.

🇲🇦 Cultural Inclusion

Built with deep local context. Full native Arabic (RTL) support ensures the platform is accessible to every professional and homeowner in Morocco.

"Morocco has incredible local talent—plumbers, electricians, cooks, nannies—but no modern way to discover them. Lbricol creates that bridge: making skilled work visible, safe, and accessible to everyone."
— Founder, Lbricol.com
