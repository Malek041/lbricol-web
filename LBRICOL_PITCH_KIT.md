**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**March 2026 | Casablanca, Morocco**

### **Lbricol Launches Morocco's First On-Demand Home Services Marketplace**
*Platform connects homeowners with vetted local professionals across 7 Moroccan cities*

**CASABLANCA, MOROCCO** — Lbricol, a Moroccan-built technology startup, has launched a mobile-first marketplace that connects households with trusted, local service professionals across Morocco. The platform — available today at **lbricol.com** — covers an extensive range of services including home repairs, cleaning, plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, gardening, babysitting, cooking, and private drivers.

#### The Problem Lbricol Solves

Morocco's 37 million inhabitants rely heavily on informal networks and word-of-mouth to find trusted home service workers. There is no standardized pricing, no background vetting, and no accountability — leaving clients vulnerable and skilled workers without sustainable income streams.

Lbricol changes this. By building a structured marketplace with real-time job distribution, transparent pricing, and a rating system, the platform creates trust on both sides of the transaction.

#### How It Works

**For Clients:**
1. Visit lbricol.com, select a city and service category
2. Browse available professionals with ratings, portfolios, and hourly rates
3. Book and pay securely — with real-time messaging and order tracking

**For Professionals ("Bricolers"):**
1. Sign up at lbricol.com/join
2. Build a profile with work photos, certifications, and hourly rates
3. Receive and accept job requests in real-time

#### Service Categories (25+)

The platform covers five major verticals:
- **Home Repairs**: Handyman, Plumbing, Electricity, Painting, Mounting, Furniture Assembly, Home Repairs
- **Cleaning & Maintenance**: Cleaning, Glass Cleaning, Pool Cleaning, Gardening
- **Care Services**: Babysitting, Elderly Care, Pets Care
- **Culinary & Cultural**: Private Chefs, Moroccan Cooking Classes, Arabic Language Lessons, Licensed Tour Guides
- **Mobility**: Private Drivers, Airport Transfers, Intercity Transport, Courier & Delivery

## 3️⃣ FOR INVESTORS — Pitch Narrative

---

### 🎯 The Opportunity in One Sentence

> **A $3B+ informal home services market in Morocco — with no dominant digital player. Lbricol is building the infrastructure to own it.**

---

### 📊 Market Context

| Metric | Data |
|--------|------|
| Morocco Population | ~37 million |
| Urban Population | ~65% (≈24M) |
| Middle Class Growth | +4.5% YoY |
| Internet Penetration | ~90% |
| Smartphone Penetration | ~85% |
| Home services TAM (Morocco) | Est. $2–4B/year (informal) |
| MENA Gig Economy CAGR | ~15% (2024–2030) |

Morocco is at an inflection point: rising urbanization, a growing middle class with dual-income households, and near-universal smartphone adoption — yet the home services sector remains entirely fragmented and informal. No Moroccan equivalent of TaskRabbit, Angi, or Urban Company exists today.

---

### 🔥 The Problem (validated by data)

1. **For Clients**: Finding a plumber, electrician, or cleaner means asking neighbors or WhatsApp groups. There's no pricing transparency, no accountability, no recourse if quality is poor.

2. **For Workers**: Skilled tradespeople and professionals have no digital presence. They depend on referral networks, earning inconsistently with no access to demand beyond their immediate circle.

3. **For the Economy**: Morocco's informal labor market represents ~40% of GDP. Formalizing even a fraction through a platform creates taxable economic activity and measurable social uplift.

---

### 💡 The Solution — Lbricol

A **two-sided marketplace** built mobile-first for Moroccan consumers:

```
Client opens app → picks service + city → 
real-time job posted → matched Bricoler accepts → 
job completed + rated → payment processed
```

**Key Differentiators:**
- 🇲🇦 Built by Moroccans, for Morocco — Darija included
- 📱 Mobile-first with offline-resilient UX
- ⭐ Trust layer: ratings, portfolios, work photos, pricing transparency
- 🔔 Real-time notification & messaging system
- 🌍 Multi-language: Arabic (RTL), French, English
- 🏙️ Already live in 7 cities, neighborhood-level matching

---

### 💰 Business Model

#### Revenue Streams

| Stream | Mechanism | Margin |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Platform Commission** | 15–20% fee on each completed job | ~80% gross margin |
| **Pro Subscription** | Featured placement + priority matching | Recurring MRR |
| **Referral Network** | Client & Bricoler referral credit system | CAC reduction |
| **Enterprise/B2B** | Facility management, hospitality sector | High-value contracts |

#### Unit Economics (illustrative)

- Average job value: ~250–500 MAD (~$25–$50)
- Platform take: ~60–90 MAD per job (~$6–$9)
- Bricoler average sessions/week: 5–10
- Monthly platform revenue per active Bricoler: ~1,200–3,600 MAD
